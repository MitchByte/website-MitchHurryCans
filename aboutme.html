About me

I am teaching Graffiti to kids and adults through school workshops and booked workshops since 2015.

Apart from my passion to show people how to paint, I do illustrations and wall painting. Beside this I am deeply curious about learning new stuff like digital design, the art of coding and creating applications. If you're looking for someone to design a wall, make a memorable workshop for you and/or your kids or any creative idea feel free to contact me.
